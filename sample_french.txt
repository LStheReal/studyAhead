Oui - Yes
Non - No
Chat - Cat
Chien - Dog
Maison - House
École - School
Livre - Book
Stylo - Pen
Ordinateur - Computer
Ami - Friend
Famille - Family
Manger - To eat
Boire - To drink
Dormir - To sleep
Aller - To go
Venir - To come

Simple Grammar Rules:
Nouns in French have a gender: masculine or feminine.
Le is for masculine, La is for feminine.
Les is for plural.
Verbs are conjugated based on the subject (Je, Tu, Il/Elle, Nous, Vous, Ils/Elles).
